# Rebuy Thrift - Zara-Inspired Design System

## 🎨 Design Philosophy
Minimalist, fashion-forward, clean, and sophisticated - inspired by Zara's aesthetic.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Black**: #000000 (Primary text, headers, buttons)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (Background, cards)
- **Gray**: #F5F5F5 (Secondary background)
- **Dark Gray**: #333333 (Secondary text)
- **Light Gray**: #E5E5E5 (Borders, dividers)

### Accent Colors
- **Red**: #D32F2F (Sale, urgent actions)
- **Success**: #2E7D32 (Success states)

## Typography

### Font Family
```css
font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;
```

### Font Sizes
- **H1**: 32px, 700 weight, letter-spacing: -0.5px
- **H2**: 24px, 600 weight, letter-spacing: -0.3px
- **H3**: 18px, 600 weight
- **Body**: 14px, 400 weight
- **Small**: 12px, 400 weight

## Layout Principles

1. **Minimal Padding**: Tight, efficient use of space
2. **Clean Lines**: Sharp borders, no rounded corners
3. **Grid System**: Strict alignment
4. **White Space**: Strategic use for breathing room
5. **Full-Width Images**: Hero sections and product images

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Black background, white text, no border-radius
- **Secondary**: White background, black border, black text
- **Hover**: Slight opacity change (0.8)

### Cards
- **No shadows**: Flat design
- **Thin borders**: 1px solid #E5E5E5
- **No border-radius**: Sharp corners

### Navigation
- **Minimal**: Thin, uppercase text
- **Fixed header**: Always visible
- **Underline on hover**: Simple interaction

### Product Cards
- **Large images**: Focus on product
- **Minimal text**: Product name, price only
- **Hover effect**: Slight zoom on image

All CSS files will be updated to match this design system.
